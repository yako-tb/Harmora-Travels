Italian Itinerary Highlights

  • Day 1-2: Rome - Visit the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Vatican City. Enjoy authentic Roman cuisine at Da Enzo al 29.
  • Day 3-4: Florence - Admire Renaissance art at Uffizi Gallery and climb the Duomo. Dine at Trattoria Zà Zà.
  • Day 5-6: Venice - Gondola ride on Grand Canal, visit St. Mark’s Square and Burano Island. Seafood & pasta at Antiche Carampane.
  • Day 7: Milan - Milan Cathedral, shopping at Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, and try panzerotti at Luini.
  • Day 8: Naples & Pompeii - Tour Pompeii ruins and savor Neapolitan pizza at L’Antica Pizzeria da Michele.
  • Day 9-10: Amalfi Coast - Relax on beaches, boat ride, and enjoy home-cooked coastal food at La Tagliata.
